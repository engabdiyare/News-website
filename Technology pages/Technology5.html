Kenya oo u digtay Shirkadda (META) Facebook

Eng. Abdiaziz

5 mins ago

3mins

Dowladda Kenya ayaa digniin u dirtay shirkadda META (Facebook) kaddib markii ay ogolaatay “Hadallo horseedi kara Nacayb & kicin, War-xumada & welwel gelinta” Dadka Dalkaas oo isu diyaarinaya Doorashada ku asteysan 9ka August 2022.


Hay’adda Ilaalisa is dhexgalka Qowmiyadaha Kenya ayaa sheegtay in muddo todobaad ah ay u qabatay maamulka Shirkadda META in ay wax ka qabato waxyaabaha keeni kara in ay is fara saaraan Dadka Dalkaas.


Kenya ayaa ku hanjabtay in haddii Shirkadda META aysan wax ka qaban cabashadooda ay ka mamnuuci doonto guud ahaan dalkaas si looga hortago ficillada kicin kara Dadka ee la xiriira ololaha Doorashada.


Kooxda u doodda Xuquuqda Aadanaha ee Global Witness fadhigeeduna yahay London ayaa sheegtay in maamulka Shirkadda META ay ka garaabeen cabashada daba socotay in ka badan 12 Xayaysiis siyaasadeed ah oo kahor imaanaya shuruucda Kenya.


Doorashada guud ee Kenya ayaa ku beegan 9ka August 2022, Shacabku waxa ay dooran doonaan Madaxweynaha, xubnaha Baarlamaanka qaranka, Senatarada & Guddoomiyaasha Ismaamullada 47da ah.


Kenya ayaa sheegtay in ay adkeyneyso nabadgalyada soohdinta ay la wadaagto Soomaaliya & Uganda oo ay uga imaadaan weerarada ugu badan, Kahor waqtiga Doorashada waxaa la xadidayaa dhaqdhaqaaqa Dadka.
